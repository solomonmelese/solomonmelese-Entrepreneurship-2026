Course Description

Entrepreneurship, now a common course for all Ethiopian freshmen, aims to foster self-employment mindsets and behavioral change. This teaching material covers seven chapters: nature of entrepreneurship, business planning, business formation, product development, marketing, financing, and business ethics.
